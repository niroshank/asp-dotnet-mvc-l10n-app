Certificate Course in Computer Technology

The course would be suitable for all levels of employees who wish to improve the day-today work using ICT as well as young adults who wish to improve ICT skills.

Objectives

  • To develop human resource with demanding ICT skills
  • To increase the employability of the participants
  • To offer the opportunity to people especially from the southern region
  • To provide the oppotunity to aquire ICT knowledge from a qualified faculty
  • To give hands-on experience in modern computer laboratories
